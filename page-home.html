About Me

Hi, I'm Elyza! Welcome to my lifestyle blog where I write about my daily adventures as an IR student and aspiring diplomat.

I'm a graduate of Consular and Diplomatic Affairs at De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde with a strong background in the volunteer work program and an advocate of children's and women's rights for eight years now. During my college years, I developed my skills in writing because I read a lot of books, and I thought of sharing my life experiences through blogging to educate and inspire people.
Inspired by the memoirs of Princess Grace Kelly of Monaco and Princess Diana, I want to make a difference through educating women and children and motivate them to become what they want to be.

I hope you join me in this journey, as I built this blog to document my life and some other things in between - in the pursuit of my goals, dreams, and aspirations for God and country!
